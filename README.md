# Epochs of Ascendancy

**A grand strategy game of empire, industry, and ascendancy across the 20th and 21st centuries.**

Built in **Godot 4.6** — inspired by *Hearts of Iron IV*, *Terra Invicta*, and *Supreme Ruler*.

---

## Vision

Epochs of Ascendancy lets you take control of a nation across three pivotal starting dates:

- **1918** – Post-WWI world of empires and revolution
- **1936** – Interwar rearmament and the road to global conflict
- **2026** – Modern multipolar world with great-power competition and advanced technology

Guide your nation through economic development, technological breakthroughs, military expansion, and ideological choices. Shape history — or rewrite it entirely.

---

## Current Features

- **Three fully playable start dates** (1918, 1936, 2026)
- **100 strategic provinces** with realistic resources, terrain, and special features
- **Dynamic province system** — factories, development level, infrastructure, population, victory points
- **Special features** with levels and icons (Research Centers, Ports, Naval Shipyards, Oil Rigs, Nuclear Plants, Spaceports, etc.)
- **Country system** with ideologies, stability, war support, capital provinces, and colors
- **Clickable map** with country-colored provinces, capital stars ⭐, and stacked special icons
- **Detailed province info panel** with resources, core claims, and special features
- **Hover effects** and responsive UI
- **Data-driven architecture** — easy to mod and expand

---

## Controls

- **Left Click** — Select province and open info panel
- **Mouse Hover** — Highlight provinces (hover pop effect)
- **Close Panel** — Click the X button or press Escape (coming soon)

---

## Systems Overview

- **Province System** — Static geography + dynamic scenario overrides
- **Country System** — Ideology, tech level, focus trees (planned), stability
- **Resource & Economy** — Factories, development, resources, infrastructure
- **Special Features** — Strategic buildings with levels and bonuses
- **Scenario System** — Three historical/modern starts with era-appropriate data
- **Modding Ready** — JSON-based data for easy province and scenario creation

## How to Run (Development)

1. Clone the repository
2. Open the project in **Godot 4.6.2** or newer
3. Open `scenes/WorldMap.tscn`
4. Press **F5** to run
